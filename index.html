A week about books

Read Post
Visit Us

This week, our toddlers will be talking about books. We will explore books, the covers, pages, pictures and words. They will also do a fun little activity where they use magnifying glasses to look through the books and the teacher will help them identify some numbers, letters, and objects.

Then, the class will make their own book by drawing and painting some pictures through out the week then putting them together.

Lastly, the children will end the week with bringing their favorite book from home to share with their friends.

Fun!
